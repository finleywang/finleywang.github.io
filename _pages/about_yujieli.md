Dr. Yujie Li is a doctoral candidate at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), affiliated with the Research Center for Intelligent Equipment Systems. His research interests include multivariate time series forecasting, foundation models for time series, and AI4Science. He is pursuing his Ph.D. under the supervision of **Prof. Yongjun Xu** and **Assoc. Prof. Fei Wang**. He has published several papers in leading journals and conferences such as KDD, AAAI, CIKM, and Pattern Recognition. As an early-stage researcher in data mining, he has already received multiple student honors, including the **National Scholarship** and annual corporate-sponsored scholarships. His independent research has accumulated nearly 50 citations on Google Scholar. In addition, he has served as a reviewer for many venues including KDD, NeurIPS, ICLR, AAAI, CIKM, Neural Networks, and Knowledge-Based Systems.
